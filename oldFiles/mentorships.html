Medical school admissions: an overview

Feeling lost? Need a mentor? Having trouble locating a tutor for your basic science course? Need a volunteer or research position? Want to shadow an actual doctor?

Any of these mentorship programs are suitable for students at any stage of their academic career, contemplating graduate school admissions. You should feel free to sign up, even if you are in the very early stages of your application process, e.g. high school senior, college freshmen, etc. The earlier you prepare, the stronger your application will be because you will have the benefit of time to make the best presentation.

Basic Mentorship

Contact us for pricing and package details

  • Live 1-hour "getting to know your profile" strategy session with advice on admissions process only: financing the application process, choice of schools to apply, “cold feet” night before interview, retake MCATs.
  • Live half-hour mock interview with verbal feedback (no audio recording) with single traditional interviewer style
  • Live half-hour mock interview in the multiple mini interview style
  • Review of work/activities on your application form
  • Review of personal statement for theme and concepts only (maximum: 2 drafts and must allow
  • Maximum: Application to ten schools two-week turnaround period for each draft)
  • Unlimited general advice (by email) that is prompt and responsive
  • Always personalized, customized attention
  • We are able to negotiate a discounted rate for select private tutors (e.g. MCAT, basic science courses, etc).
  • Premedical student course selection
  • Last minute efforts to top out the GPA
  • Negotiating discounts with private tutors for basic science courses
  • Search for scholarship opportunities and discuss source of funds for tuition upon admissions; for more comprehensive post-admissions coaching, please enroll in our “Scholarships and Loans” package, once you’ve been accepted
  • Access to widening network of premedical students, medical students, residents in training, and practicing physicians for more customized questions, e.g. interest in particular training programs and schools
  • Brainstorming on the best use of the time in a gap year
  • Proper professional etiquette (interviews, asking recommenders for letters, addressing admissions committee, etc.)
  • Coping with stress (college courses, MCAT test-taking, application process, etc.)

Comprehensive Mentorship

Contact us for pricing and package details

  • Live 2-hour “getting to know your profile” strategy session with advice on anything related to doctoring: includes process before, during and after admissions, nontraditional physician careers (military, pharmaceuticals, legal experts, financial consultants, etc.), scholarships for schools, post-residency life
  • Live 2-hour mock interviews with written feedback (audio recording included) with two different traditional interviewer styles. The hours may need to be scheduled on separate days, according to our interviewer’s availability.
  • Live 1-hour mock interview in the multiple mini interview style
  • Review of work/activities on your application form
  • Edit personal statement for theme, concepts, and English (max: 2 drafts and must allow two-week turnaround period for each draft)
  • Maximum: Application to twenty schools
  • Letters of recommendation (review)
  • Review of Secondary Essays for content and English (max: 4 drafts)
  • School list
  • Letter of intent
  • Post-interview advice
  • School choice decisions

  • Some of the unpaid clinical internship or research positions offer exclusive access to physicians and academicians at hospitals and teaching institutions. This is an unique, front-row opportunity to get hands on experience AND establish lasting relationships with highly esteemed physician mentors.

1. There will be no refunds, partial or complete, once services have begun to be rendered. If you decide midway through the application process that you are not interested anymore after all, we can discuss whether it is feasible to apply the remainder of your unused services for applying to schools of another discipline
2. Unfortunately, we do not provide “a la carte” services where you can pick and choose to subscribe to a specific service, e.g. essay edits, mock interviews. We provide comprehensive packages because the best way to help you is to polish every aspect of your application. We want to maximize your chances of admissions. Being involved in only bits and pieces deters us from discovering potential weaknesses in your application. Your success is key to our success!
3. Much of this mentorship can be achieved remotely, if geographic location bars you from in-person meetings. So if you are not physically located in New York City but are still interested in enrolling my assistance, please contact us anyway, so we can discuss possible options.
