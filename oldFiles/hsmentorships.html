Comprehensive Mentorship

Contact us for pricing and package details

  • Live 2-hour “getting to know your profile” strategy session with advice on anything related to admissions and career paths: choice of undergraduate schools to apply, geographical ideals, “cold feet” night before interview, retake SATs, career paths and options, includes process before, during and after admissions, career choices, scholarships for schools, financing the application process.
  • Live 2-hour mock interviews with written feedback (audio recording included) with two different interviewer styles. The hours may need to be scheduled on separate days, according to our interviewer’s availability.
  • Review of work/activities on your application form
  • Edit personal statement for theme, concepts, and proper English (max: 2 drafts and must allow two-week turnaround period for each draft)
  • Letters of recommendation (review)
  • Maximum: Application to ten schools
  • School list
  • Letter of intent
  • Post-interview advice
  • School choice decisions
  • Useful for NYC students interested in pursuing health professions: As opportunities become available, you will receive email updates on private volunteer and research positions in medical institutions throughout NYC. Some of the unpaid clinical internship or research positions offer exclusive access to physicians and academicians at hospitals and teaching institutions. This is a unique, front-row opportunity to get hands on experience AND establish lasting relationships with highly esteemed physician mentors.
  • Useful for NYC high school students wanting to excel in certain courses: Link to private and commercial tutors will be made available after personalized selection for quality. We offer to negotiate discounts with private tutors (e.g. SAT, HS courses, etc).
  • Unlimited general advice (by email) that is prompt and responsive
  • Always personalized, customized attention
  • Selection in elective courses and extra-curricular activities
  • Last minute efforts to top out the GPA
  • Search for scholarship opportunities and discuss source of funds for tuition upon admissions; for more comprehensive post-admissions coaching, please enroll in our “Scholarships and Loans” package, once you’ve been accepted
  • Brainstorming on the best use of time on weekends, afterschool hours, and during summers
  • Proper professional etiquette (interviews, asking recommenders for letters, addressing admissions committee, etc.)
  • Coping with stress (high school courses, SAT test-taking, application process, etc.)
  • The “encore” bonus: If you don’t get admitted to the school of your choice on your first try and want to reapply the following year, Dr. Kuo will hold your hand the second time for free! Nothing makes her prouder than a student who achieves success.

1. There will be no refunds, partial or complete, once services have begun to be rendered. If you decide midway through the application process that you are not interested anymore after all, we can discuss whether it is feasible to apply the remainder of your unused services for applying to schools of another discipline
2. Unfortunately, we do not provide “a la carte” services where you can pick and choose to subscribe to a specific service, e.g. essay edits, mock interviews. We provide comprehensive packages because the best way to help you is to polish every aspect of your application. We want to maximize your chances of admissions. Being involved in only bits and pieces deters us from discovering potential weaknesses in your application. Your success is key to our success!
3. Much of this mentorship can be achieved remotely, if geographic location bars you from in-person meetings. So if you are not physically located in New York City but are still interested in enrolling my assistance, please contact us anyway, so we can discuss possible options.
