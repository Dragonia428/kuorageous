It is never too late to become a doctor! Whether you are a post-baccalaureate, a college senior planning a gap year, or someone on his/her second career, I understand firsthand the trials and tribulations of being an untraditional student. I also have witnessed the challenges and triumphs of those who pursued medicine as a second career and others who gained worldly experience for a few years prior to applications. Allow me to advise you and to potentially connect you with similarly minded people who have now achieved success!
